Chicken Marsala

Classic rustic italian recipe.

This classic dish is creamy and hearty as well as filling. Start with 1 pound of chicken breasts(butterfiled and pounded). Coat the chicken with flour and set aside. Heat one skillet with olive olive until smoking. Cook chicken breasts(2 - 3 minutes each side). Set Chicken to the side for now. In a seperate skillet, heat 2 Tbsp of Olive oil until hot.
