Chicken Marsala

Classic rustic italian recipe.

This classic dish is creamy and hearty, as well as filling.
It is best served with spaghetti with a side of the Marsala sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Chicken Breast
  • 2Tbsp Olive Oil
  • Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Marsal Wine

Steps

  1. Heat the Olive oil and cook chicken
  2. In sepearte pan, add olive oil garlic, shallots, and mushrooms
  3. Once both cooked, throw in one pan and add marsala wine
  4. Let simmer for 10 minutes
